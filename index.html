About Us

CRYN is founded by Farjad Ahmed Yousufi to empower young people across Pakistan to tackle climate challenges and build sustainable communities. We organize projects, workshops, awareness campaigns, and interactive learning experiences to foster resilience against climate change.

Youth Engagement

We involve hundreds of young people in local climate initiatives and projects.

Environmental Projects

Plantation drives, cleanups, and community-based sustainability programs.

Workshops & Training

Interactive sessions to teach climate resilience and sustainable practices.

Projects

Workshops for students to understand climate resilience.
